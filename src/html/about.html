https://brokenscreen.online

Donald Hanson, 2018

Part screen-saver part screen unsaved. https://brokenscreen.online is an abstract live render artpiece made with JavaScript. The scene consists of four actors. Four cubes- two wireframe and two opaque. The wire cubes slowly form patterns in the background as the opaque cubes leave a trail of pixels wherever they go. Made with LiveRender AV, an audiovisual framework created by the artist, Three.js and WebMIDI.js.

Available for live performance as a VJ/AV set. Domain/artpiece available for purchase from the artist.
